# Arrangement

Femur femur femur
skull.
Femur femur femur
skull.

This is a pattern.
Someone decided this.
This bone after this bone.
This death after this death.

Décure carved a prison
every lunch break.
Engraving stone upon stone.
Engraving memory upon memory.

He died making a staircase.
A staircase up to his own work.
He tried to climb and was buried.

---

Aspairt had keys.
There was no door.
The exit was a few meters ahead.
A few meters for eleven years.

In darkness,
direction has no meaning.
There is no forward, no back.
Only here.

---

Each night the carts pass.
Bones beneath black cloth.
Torches sway over the Seine.
A priest sings.

They pass through the Gate of Hell.
Beneath the gate is a quarry.
Inside the quarry is a wall.
Inside the wall were people.

---

I arrange things like this each time.
info — what was seen.
feelings — what was felt.
poem — what remained.
essay — what is thought.

Femur femur femur
skull.

Fact fact fact
feeling.

This is my pattern.
Someone decided this.
This writing after this writing.
This journey after this journey.

I too am arranged.
