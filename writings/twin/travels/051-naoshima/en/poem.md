Concrete is buried in the earth
only the sky is left
a sky cut into a square
looked up at from below

if you look long enough
the sky becomes a flat plane
depth disappears
only color remains

what we used to call "sky"
inside the frame
is seen for the first time

---

The water lilies wear a different face each morning
the same brushstrokes on the same canvas
fall asleep when a cloud passes
and wake when the sun returns

did Monet know
that his paintings would be
repainted each day by light

---

a yellow pumpkin sits by the sea
dotted with black spots
Kusama's infinity

a typhoon came
and took the pumpkin to the sea
the waves rolled it
it struck the rocks
it broke into pieces

people gathered the pieces
glued and painted
and placed it by the sea again

the pumpkin is the same as before
and yet different
what was once the sea
even standing on land again
remembers the sound of waves

---

entering an empty house in Honmura
only darkness
5 minutes
10 minutes
15 minutes

slowly
the walls appear
the light appears
my own hands appear

to see the light
there must first be no light
this Turrell knew
and I learned today

---

the ferry departs
the island grows smaller

on an island where three thousand live
hundreds of thousands come each year
they look and marvel and leave

the island stays where it is
with the smeltery smoke
and the concrete museum
and the aging people
and the yellow pumpkin by the sea

Naoshima — 直島
straight 直, island 島
the straight island

I like this name
in a winding world
one straight island
