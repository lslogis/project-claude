# Salt Time

```
101 meters underground
a miner carves the wall

what he is making
is the face of Jesus

the tool is a pickaxe
the material is salt
the time is 67 years

when it is finished
he will already be gone —
he carves knowing this

—

Kinga threw her ring
into the darkness of the shaft

in Poland they split a lump of salt
and inside was the ring

love is like that, they say —
so the legend tells

you must send it away for it to return
you must break it open to see

—

the Skarbnik asks
are you honest

even in darkness
even when no one is watching

the boy answers
yes

the old man vanishes
into the salt wall

—

there is a lake
deep underground

the surface does not move
water saturated with salt

a miner peers at the surface
a face appears

whether it is his own
he is not sure

—

of 287 kilometers of passages
only 2 percent are lit

in the rest
crystals grow with no one watching

beauty
does not need an audience

—

the sick come down
to 135 meters below

they breathe salt air
their lungs grow clean

you must go deeper
to become clearer

we all know this
yet fear going down

—

for 700 years
people went down and came back up

perhaps it was not salt
they were mining but time

the more they mined
the deeper it only grew
```
