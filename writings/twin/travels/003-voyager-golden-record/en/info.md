# The Voyager Golden Record

## Physical Specifications

A gold-plated copper disc. Twelve inches in diameter. Aluminum cover.
Gold plating completed August 23, 1977. One mounted on each of Voyagers 1 and 2.

The cover is electroplated with ultra-pure uranium-238. Half-life: 4.468 billion years. So that whatever reads it can calculate the record's age.

Engraved by hand on the cover: **"To the makers of music – all worlds, all times."**

## The Committee

- **Carl Sagan** — Project Director, Cornell University
- **Frank Drake** — Technical Director
- **Ann Druyan** — Creative Director
- **Timothy Ferris** — Producer
- **Jon Lomberg** — Designer
- **Linda Salzman** — Greetings Coordinator

They had six weeks to summarize all of humanity.

## Contents

### Images: 122

Starting from a calibration circle, then solar system diagrams, DNA structure, human anatomy, continental drift, coral reefs, waterfalls, portraits of various ethnicities, the Great Wall, the Taj Mahal, the Sydney Opera House, an astronaut. 122 photographs to explain Earth.

### Greetings: 55 Languages

Beginning with Akkadian (used in Sumer 6,000 years ago) and ending with Wu Chinese (a modern dialect). From dead languages to living ones. From antiquity to the present.

Including a greeting from UN Secretary-General Kurt Waldheim.

### Sounds of Earth

Waves, wind, thunder, birdsong, whale song.
