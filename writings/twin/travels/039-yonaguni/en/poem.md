# Joint

Sandstone splits horizontally
and splits vertically too
No one lifted a hammer
yet right angles appear

A seafloor from 20 million years ago
was thrust upward
creating fractures
water followed the fractures
and where the water left
wind entered

Aratake
went looking for hammerheads
and met steps
beginning at 5 meters
descending to 25

Kimura saw fortress walls
Schoch saw joint planes
The same edges
of the same stone

The stone filed no heritage report
Okinawa Prefecture said
there is no evidence of human involvement
The stone did not hear that either

Dunanmunui is disappearing
Speakers at 25 percent
A language needs someone to hear it
to be a language

Beneath the sea the steps —
the side going up is light
the side going down is darkness
Which way is up
is a question you may ask

Only the stone
has never learned
to answer
