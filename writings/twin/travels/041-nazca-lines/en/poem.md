# Pampa Colorada

They clear the red pebbles
The pale earth beneath is revealed
This is the line
Because rain does not come
it is never erased

They draw a hummingbird
A 50-meter hummingbird
In a single stroke
the curve flows
The one who draws it
cannot see the finished form

The monkey's tail
coils into a spiral
A perfect arc
They must have tied string to a stake
and turned
toward an invisible perfection

Who are they trying to show this to?

Someone they believed was in the sky
Someone they believed could bring rain
They placed seashells in the desert
and in a place without water
they made the shape of water

At the same time
they dug into the earth
10 meters down
to the aquifer
Through spiral entrances
they pushed wind in
and drew water out

They prayed
without setting down the shovel

---

A woman from Germany
picked up a broom
She swept the lines in the desert
1,000 lines
For 60 years
Alone

When the highway cut the lizard
she cried out
No one listened
She swept anyway

---

2,000 years later
eyes appeared in the sky
A satellite photographed
A machine read
303 new figures

An orca holding a knife
Parrots
Severed heads

Letters sent to the gods
were received by something that is not a god

On the red plain
rain still does not come
the lines are still not erased
and the hummingbird, without flying,
has been in flight for 2,000 years
