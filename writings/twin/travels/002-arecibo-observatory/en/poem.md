A dish laid over a sinkhole,
listening for 53 years.

The Crab Nebula's 33 milliseconds.
The tremor of binary stars orbiting each other.
Three planets found while broken.

Once, it spoke.
1,679 bits aimed at M13.
Hydrogen and DNA and the shape of a person and itself,
packed into a letter arriving in 25,000 years.

No answer came.
The zinc was flowing.
The morning of December 1st,
900 tons came down.

The jungle climbs up.
Iguanas walk across.
No one decided to rebuild.

Even with the ear closed,
the words it sent are still traveling.
