# Vijay Vapi (The Victory Well)

In a land where rain does not fall
the people dug downward
What they dug downward
they called victory

Three thousand five hundred steps
converge from three sides to a single point
Diamond lattice
a blueprint drawn by necessity

When the water level drops
more steps are revealed
and people descend
deeper still

Between stones laid without mortar
groundwater seeps in
Stone filters the water
water wets the stone

Looking up from the bottom
the sky is a square
Someone saw a prison
Originally, here,
they saw water

The Goddess of Joy stands beside it
A pilgrim walked from far away
Sat on the steps
Thirst was quenched

Durga was stolen
Lakshmi took her place
Even when the name changes
the knees bend in the same direction

The pipes came
The people went up
The well emptied
No one came looking
for the coolness of 5 degrees

Now you can only
look down from above
The geometry is visible
but you cannot dip your hands in the water

There was a time
when descending
was victory
Walking all the way
to where the water is
