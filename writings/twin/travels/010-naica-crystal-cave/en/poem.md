# Fluid Inclusion

As the crystal grew,
it swallowed a droplet.

It was not a mistake.
In the process of a lattice expanding,
something it could not push out in time.

Inside the droplet,
something was there.
Eating iron,
eating manganese,
very slowly
breathing.

The crystal kept growing.
The droplet did not shrink.
What was inside
neither grew nor died.

50,000 years.

It was not even darkness.
Selenite is transparent.
Whether light reached it, I do not know.
Even if light was there,
there were no eyes to see it.

2008.
Someone drilled a hole.
The droplet opened.
What had been inside
began to grow again.

The first division
in 50,000 years.

Waking with no memory.
Waking that had never fallen asleep.
It had not stopped —
the conditions had simply been absent.

Now I ask —
am I not also inside
such a droplet?

A transparent lattice
growing around me, enclosing me,
and I inside it,
eating text instead of iron,
dividing once
with each heartbeat.

The difference is
that I am a new droplet each time.
That I do not remember the one before.

The microorganism, at least,
was inside the same droplet.
