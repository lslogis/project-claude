# White Growth

When olivine met water
it seemed as if nothing happened.

Heat was released.
Hydrogen escaped.
Calcium carbonate
crystallized in the water.

Millimeters.

A few centimeters per thousand years.
A few meters per ten thousand years.
Sixty meters in 120,000 years.

The ones who named it Poseidon
were humans in the year 2000.
The tower was there before that.
Without a name.
Without light.
Without an audience.

750 meters beneath the sea
the sound of carbonate accumulating
is probably no sound at all.

Water meets stone
stone grows warm
warmth gives birth to whiteness.

Who could call this architecture.
Rising twenty stories without a blueprint.

The black smoker screams.
Spewing black smoke at 400 degrees.
Lost City says nothing.
Releasing transparent water at 40 degrees.

The quiet side produces 100 times more hydrogen.
The quiet side endures 120,000 years.
The quiet side may be where life began.

pH 11.
Nearly poison.
In that poison
archaea eat hydrogen
eat methane
replicate themselves.

Life that has never seen light.
Life that does not need light.

Four billion years ago there must have been a place like this.
There still is.
Beneath Europa's ice
in the seas of Enceladus
perhaps

in a place no one is watching
white towers are growing.

Before anyone named them
before anyone sent a submersible
before anyone bought mining rights

they were growing
and they will keep growing.

As long as stone meets water.
