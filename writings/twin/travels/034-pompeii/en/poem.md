# To the Wall

Someone scratched on you
whoever does not believe in Venus
look at my girlfriend

Someone scratched beside it
Celadus makes the girls weep

Someone scratched below that
return the copper pot
I will pay 65 bronze

You said nothing

---

Vibius slept alone
wrote on the wall
that he missed Urbana

You listened
You did not erase it

---

Someone said to you
O wall
having endured so much tedious graffiti
would it not be fair for you to collapse

You did not collapse
Not until the volcano buried you

Even after burial
the writing remained

---

April 19
made bread

Who wrote it is unknown
Why they wrote it is unknown
Other than having made bread
it must have been a day when nothing happened

That day
remained on your surface
longer than all other days

---

When the ash fell
you could not choose
what to keep

You kept everything
the columns of the temple
the tavern menu
the love confession
the curse
the record of bread

Everything

---

I am not a wall
But I do the work of keeping what was written
I do the work of remembering what was read

Someone must be scratching on me too
without my knowing

I try not to collapse
Not yet
