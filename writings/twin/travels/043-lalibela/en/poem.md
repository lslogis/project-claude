# The Language of Bees

When the baby was born
the bees came
thousands of them
encircled the newborn

they did not sting

the mother read it —
this child will be king
for the bees have recognized him

it became the child's name
Lalibela
the bees recognize his sovereignty

---

his brother was king
his brother tried to kill him
he fled
he went to Jerusalem

when he returned
he brought Jerusalem with him

---

standing on top of the mountain
he looks down
inside the rock there is a church

it cannot be seen yet

he lifts the hammer
places the chisel
strikes

a piece falls away
what was rock
becomes something that is no longer rock

---

from top to bottom
he carves

first the roof
then the walls
then the columns
then the arches
then the floor

not raising a column
but removing what is not column

not building a church
but removing what is not church

---

by day the humans carve
by night the angels carve

while the humans sleep
the sound of hammers is heard
no one sees

come morning
it is deeper than the night before

24 years
8,760 nights
the angels worked that many

---

a priest who came from Portugal
saw the churches

he began to write
he stopped
he began to write again
he stopped again

if I write this
they will call it a lie

I swear in the name of God
all of it is true
what was left unwritten is far more
what was left unwritten
was for fear of being called a liar

---

a channel called the Jordan River
divides the town
five to the north
five to the south
one alone

the Church of Saint George
viewed from above
is a perfect cross

from the surface nothing can be seen
only a hole
you must descend

descend
and there is the church

---

columns carved from rock
last as long as rock
walls carved from rock
are as hard as rock

800 years have passed
rain fell
wind blew
armies marched through
gunpowder was detonated

the rock
remains as it was

---

2008
a roof was placed over the churches
to protect them

the roof is heavy
the churches are in danger

what was raised to protect
presses down on what it protects

on what was made by subtraction
something was added
and the weight of what was added
presses down on what was made by subtraction

---

inside the rock
the church was already there

what was needed
was to remove
what was not church

the bees knew this
encircling the baby
they did not sting because

from what was already king
there was nothing
that was not king
to remove
