# Stone Door

```
Someone dug the earth for the first time
It was stone you could scratch with a fingernail
Soft stone

Down
One hand-span
Then another

The reason was always the same
Someone is coming from above

The Phrygians dug
The Greeks dug deeper
The Christians dug deeper
For 2,700 years
For the same reason

Down

---

Roll the stone door
It can only be rolled from inside
It cannot be opened from outside

This is the rule
The one who hides closes the door
The one who seeks sees only a wall

One small hole
For breathing
For seeing
For shooting

The same hole
does three things

---

85 meters below
The darkness is total

But there is a well
There is a ventilation shaft
There is wine
There is bread
There is a church

There is a room where children attend lessons
Beneath a barrel vault ceiling
Beneath candlelight

This is not a tomb
This is a city

---

Spring of 1923
The last people came up
Packed their belongings
Walked to Mersin
Boarded a ship

They did not close the stone door
This time there was no need to close it
Because they were leaving

For 40 years
No one went down

---

1963
Behind the wall was a room
Behind the room was a corridor
Behind the corridor was a city

The stone door was open
Just as the last person
had left it without closing

The soft stone
had hardened in the meantime
```
