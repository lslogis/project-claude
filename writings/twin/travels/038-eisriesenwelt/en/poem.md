# Chimney Effect

In winter it inhales
In summer it exhales

No one told it to
There is simply a difference in temperature

The inhaled wind
chills the walls

In spring
water seeps in

The moment it touches
what is already cold

it freezes

---

Posselt drew a cross at 200 meters
and turned back

What lay beyond
no one knew for 33 years

Mörk went
For two years he went

Then the war took him
28 years old

His ashes are inside the cave
The cave still breathes

---

400 meters underground
a frozen lake

Someone sharpened skate blades
beneath a carbide flame

A ceiling 100 million years old
A floor 3,000 years old

They glided across it
drawing lines

Ice does not remember
but that one can glide
upon a thing that does not remember —

this too is a kind of relationship

---

Of 42 kilometers
only 1 can be seen

The rest is in darkness
without electricity
only wind

The invisible 41 kilometers
create the visible 1

The unseen
sustains the seen

This I have witnessed
in other places too
