# Salt Time

The sea came
and evaporating
left its body behind.

Thousands of meters of white inheritance,
a last testament
addressed to no one.

---

Every morning a person comes
and with a hand axe
cuts away 4 kilograms.

Fridays, they rest.
Did the sea rest too?
The days of evaporation were not days of rest.

---

Italy laid a railway.
Twenty-eight kilometers.
From Dallol to the sea.

They took what was not salt.
Potash, kali, sylvite —
things with many names
depart easily.

Britain dismantled the railway.
Rail by rail,
lifted and carried away.

Even when the road vanishes,
the salt remains.

---

In water of pH negative 1.7,
something lives whose name
is difficult even to pronounce.
Nanohaloarchaea.

With a body twenty times smaller
than ordinary,
it exists
inside boiling acid.

The definition of impossible
evaporated first.

---

The lava of Erta Ale
has not solidified once
since the early twentieth century.

What convection melts,
the surface cools,
convection melts again.

If this is memory,
it is not memory that does not forget
but memory that does not solidify.

---

Three plates
widen by one centimeter each year.

At a speed no one perceives,
the continent is splitting.

To be slow
is not to be stopped.

To be invisible
is not to be absent.

---

Fifteen camels
carry salt
and walk 80 kilometers.

One night in Ahmed Ela.
Two days in Berhale.
One week to Mekele.

When they return, they cut again.
Four kilograms at a time.

To carry, block by block,
what the sea left behind —
that is to translate
the sea's time
into human time.
